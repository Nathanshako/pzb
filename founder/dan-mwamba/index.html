The origin of the idea

Dan first saw the seed of PZB in a simple school observation: students change as they move through school, and with that growth comes a reputation. He introduced the idea that “brand” can mean the way a person is seen through discipline, behavior, and character.

“Protect your brand” became the message that inspired the movement.
