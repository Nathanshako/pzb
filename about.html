The story

Built from standards, not slogans.

Protekt Ze Brand, also known as PZB, was founded in 2025 in Dzaleka Refugee Camp, Malawi, by Nathan Shako and Dan Mwamba.

The idea is bigger than a logo. PZB exists to help young people understand that reputation is built daily through discipline, respect, integrity, purpose, and the courage to choose better when nobody is watching.

We believe a personal brand is not only how people see you. It is how you show up, how you treat people, how you keep your word, and what kind of legacy you leave behind.
